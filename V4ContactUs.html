    Contact Us

    Have a question, looking to sell, or searching for something special? Get in touch with us anytime. We’re always excited to connect with fellow collectors and help you find exactly what you’re after. Reach out and let’s keep the culture alive together through music, media, style, and shared passion.

    Our Story

    FOUNDED BY: College friends who met in guitar class and worked for years behind the counters of Downtown Silver Spring's Record Stores and Guitar Shops, we’ve always lived and breathed music. As avid music lovers and musicians ourselves, the hunt for vinyl, gear, and nostalgia never really stopped — it just grew. Now, we’re bringing that passion to vendor markets across the DMV area, curating a mix of modern, retro and vintage treasures: records and turntables, movies and video games, clothing, collectibles, and plenty more. Our mission is simple — to share the joy of timeless music, movies, games, and classic culture with the community that inspires us.
